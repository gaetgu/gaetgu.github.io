# Random Post number One

I might post this kind of article from time to time, whenever I feel like it. I thought it was cool how the place that I live could go from this

![image]({{site.baseurl}}/img/banking_with_mesas_in_foreground.png)


to this

![image]({{site.baseurl}}/img/from_hyde_towards_sandia.png)

in just a matter of two days. 

I live in and next to the mountains, so when I go flying there are a lot of gorges and mesas.
I also live in the high desert, so it will occasionally snow even this close to summertime (I live in the northern hemisphere). That means that I can go on a drive up onto the mountains and take a hike with beautiful mountains and snowy landscapes.

It really is beautiful where I live, that is all.
